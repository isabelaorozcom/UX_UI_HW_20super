Helping users with stomach issues live more consistently comfortable and pain-free lives by providing an app to make informed nutritional decisions.

Overview

Tummy is a mobile app designed to help People with stomach issues make informed healthy choices and ultimately, feel better

Product:App

My Role: UX Designer

Tools:Figma, Miro, Google Sheets

Understanding The User

Through a study we looked to understand how people who had stomach issues informed decisions on eating habits and identify what problems they had and what tools, solutions they used to make those issues better.

Research Primary Questions:

  • What stomach issues they had?
  • Have they been diagnosed or treated for those issues? 
  • How had technology helped them improve those problems? 
  • What they liked/disliked about those tools?

Understanding the Problem

Using an affinity diagram we were able to identify the main pain points, remedies and restrictions our users had when looking to improve stomach related issues. Amongst those the most recurrent ones were the following:

  • The main symptoms of discomfort was bloating 
  • Users have tried multiple remedies 
  • Users have difficult relationships with food because they felt everything impacted their stomach 
  • Users would default to remove some foods from diet but sometimes that didn’t work either.
  • Users were interested in mobile apps that could help them improve their condition

Analyzing existing solutions

We ran a competitive analysis to identify key threats and opportunity areas for the solution we wanted to offer.

Defining the MVP

Product vision and solution

Enable users to accomplish 3 main tasks:

  • Track their symptoms
  • Track what they ate
  • Track how they felt

The app would run correlations and identify trends with time in order to create a list of foods for the user to avoid or watch-out for in the future. The process to add foods, symptoms and mood had to be easy and quick to prevent burnout and loose adoption of the app. Additional features for subsequent iterations would be adding a list of home remedies based on the data input.
